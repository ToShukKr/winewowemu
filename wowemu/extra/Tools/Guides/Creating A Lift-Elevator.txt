Open gameobjects.scp in your Scripts folder 
Press CTRL+F on your keyboard 
Type: vator 
Press Enter 
find the gameobject id, example: [GAMEOBJ XXX] where XXX is the ID you want. 
Also then after doing the first search you can just PRESS F3 to get the rest. 

Ingame: 
GM Commands to use: 
.addgo gameobjectID 

If you make a mistake or spawn the wrong object, you can do this: 
Stand as close to the object as possbile: 
.targetgo 
.del
